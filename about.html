Our Team

Arpita Das is the Founder-Publisher of Gojek One. She is an alumna of St. Stephens College, University of Delhi and School of African and Oriental Studies, London. Arpita leads the Gojek One Workshops Series for Authors and Editors and is a Member of the Executive Board of PublisHer (www.womeninpublishing.org). She is also visiting faculty at Ashoka University. Arpita writes on book culture, gender, popular culture and bibliotherapy. Her most recent essay 'Jaipur Literature Festival, the Gendered Literary Field, and the MeToo Movement in India' was published in Memoires du Livre/Studies in Book Culture journal.

Arpita Das

Founder

Ishita Gupta is the Senior Editor and Rights Manager at Gojek One. She is an alumna of Jesus and Mary College, the University of Delhi where she completed her bachelor's and master's in psychology. She later attended Oxford Brookes University where she was awarded a master's degree in publishing. Ishita was shortlisted for the Editor of the Year Award at the Publishing Next Awards 2020. She also oversees design at Gojek One.

Ishita Gupta

Senior Editor and Rights Manager

Tanya Singh is the Editor and Development Manager at Gojek One. She attended Miranda House and Jesus and Mary College, the University of Delhi where she obtained her bachelor's and master's in English literature, respectively. A published poet, she writes on queer love and sexuality and has contributed to HelterSkelter, Juked, decomP and GAYSI. Apart from her editorial work, Tanya manages the Gojek One Workshops, The Yodakin Podcast and the Gojek One Newsletter and oversees publicity and promotions at Gojek One.

Tanya Singh
